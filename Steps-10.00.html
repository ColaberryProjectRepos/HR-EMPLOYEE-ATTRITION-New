Create Refresh Date Table 10

                                   


Detailed Instruction

1.    On Power BI Desktop ribbon, Click on Transform data.
2.    Click on the New Source button and select Blank query from the list.
3.    On the Dax formula bar type =Date Time.LocalNow() 
4.    Under properties in the Name box type in Refresh Date
5.    Click on Convert to Table button. 
6.    After the refresh table pops up, rename the column by right-clicking and choosing rename from the selection.
7.    Type Date Time
8.    Click on Close & apply button and save.
