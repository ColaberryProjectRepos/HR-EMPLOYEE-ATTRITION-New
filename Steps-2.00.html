Problem Statement 2

Employee attrition is a significant challenge that can have devastating consequences for organizations. High attrition rates (with industry averages of 20-30% and a high rate of 23% for employees aged 35 and below) can result in significant challenges for organizations, including skyrocketing recruitment and training costs, plummeting productivity, and a demoralized workforce. The loss of valuable employees can also lead to a loss of institutional knowledge and experience, making it challenging for organizations to maintain their competitive edge. Without effective intervention, the problem of employee attrition can spiral out of control, leading to long-term financial instability and even organizational failure. Therefore, it is imperative that organizations take a proactive approach to analyzing employee attrition data and developing effective retention strategies to ensure the long-term success and sustainability of their business.
